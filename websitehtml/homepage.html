Volleyball is an amazing sport played by millions around the world. Both a casual game and professional sport, learning how to play volleyball can be a gateway for a fun hobby or a potential profession. A general understanding of volleyball will be explained in this website!

  • Understand the Basics: Before diving into the specifics of gameplay, it's essential to familiarize yourself with the basic rules and terminology of volleyball:

  • Court Dimensions: A standard volleyball court measures 18 meters long and 9 meters wide, with a net running down the middle at a height of 2.43 meters for men and 2.24 meters for women.

  • Teams: Volleyball is typically played with two teams of six players each, although variations exist for different formats of the game.

  • Scoring: Points are scored when the ball lands in the opposing team's court, or when the opposing team commits a fault. Matches are typically played to 25 points, with teams needing to win by at least two points.

  • Basic Skills: Mastering essential skills such as serving, passing, setting, attacking, and blocking is crucial for success in volleyball.
